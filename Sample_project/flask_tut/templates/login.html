Crime Dashboard - India

Welcome to the Crime Dashboard, providing insights into crime statistics across 28 states and 5 union territories of India. The dashboard covers key crime categories, including auto thefts, murders, rapes, and kidnappings.

This project aims to shed light on the prevalent crime trends, helping users understand the distribution and intensity of crimes in different regions. The data presented here is sourced from reliable sources and is presented in an accessible and informative manner.

Explore the visualizations and charts to gain valuable insights into the crime landscape of India. The dashboard is designed to facilitate a comprehensive understanding of crime patterns, enabling informed decision-making and policy development.

Thank you for visiting the Crime Dashboard - India. Your awareness and engagement contribute to the collective effort in addressing and preventing crime across the nation.
